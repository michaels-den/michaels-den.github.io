Basics

Often times developers brag about characteristics of their langauge of choice. Usually this bragging is just a strange way of combining opinion with strengths/weaknesses of ways of doing things.

It might be safe to say that until we've all agreed on one language, we can't (and shouldn't) argue that one language is better than another. Some languages might be better certain things, but then we should be specific about which things.

Also, "better" inherently means "better for me" - remember that your way of doing things may not be better for others.
